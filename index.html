Why contractors use this

Clarity before you commit capital or credibility

Growth decisions in construction are rarely just about sales. They are about whether your license, cash, and crew can carry the next job without putting the business at risk.

  • Know your limit before the board asks

    Tennessee licensing ties contract limits to financial strength. A quick estimate helps you avoid surprises at renewal or when bidding above your current cap.

  • See cash risk before payroll week

    Upcoming tools will map billing cycles, retainage, and labor cost so you can spot shortfalls weeks ahead instead of after suppliers call.

  • Price jobs with margin in mind

    Profitability and burden calculators are on the roadmap so you can sanity-check bids against real labor and overhead, not gut feel alone.

  • Stay independent and practical

    No sales funnel, no lead forms in the preview. Just straightforward math you can take to your accountant, surety agent, or bonding partner.
